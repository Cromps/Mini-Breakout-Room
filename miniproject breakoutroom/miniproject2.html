Solution

Couldn't beat the game? Use the answers below to assist you in your journey!


Step 1

By inspecting the 8th cupboard in the game, you'll be able to find scissors.
After that, by clicking on the scissors, you'll be able to get out of the ropes that bind you.

Step 2

You'll need to inspect the bed to obtain the photo.
After this, there are two things you simultaneously need to do.

Step 3

You'll need to use the photo on the blackboard, which will place it in an
equation with the other picture.Then you also need to use the rope on the window to
enter the fake room, which will retrieve the safe.

Step 4

The only action you take is with the safe. To figure out the code,
you need to use the clock and the blackboard as hints. The clock has its hands on 12 and 4,
and the pictures give you the number 5, hence altogether you get a code of 1245.

Step 5

Using the screwdriver in the safe, you need to take apart the clock.
You'll find a hidden wrench in there.

Step 6

There are two places to use the screw.
If you inspect the safe, you'll notice there are bolts in it. Using the screw, you can remove them,
leading a hidden compartment with a lockpick in it.

Step 7

Finally, you need to use both the wrench and the lockpick on the door to remove its locks,
leading to your escape.
